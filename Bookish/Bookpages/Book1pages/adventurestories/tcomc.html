The Count of Monte Cristo

Alexandre Dumas & Auguste Maquet

Rate this classic

Description

A masterpiece of adventure, The Count of Monte Cristo tells the story of Edmond Dantès. Wrongfully imprisoned on his wedding day, he spends years in the dismal Chateau d'If before making a daring escape. Armed with a massive fortune and a cold heart, he returns to Paris to systematically dismantle the lives of the men who betrayed him.
