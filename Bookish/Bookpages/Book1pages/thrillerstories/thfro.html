About this Story

This is a Cold War military thriller about a high-stakes submarine chase between the United States and the Soviet Union.

The story centers on a brand-new Soviet nuclear submarine called the Red October. Its captain, Marko Ramius, suddenly turns the vessel toward the United States. The Soviet government believes he has gone rogue and orders their navy to destroy the submarine before it reaches American waters.
