About this Book

Welcome to Nocticadia, a dark academia gothic romance where the air is thick with the scent of decay and the secrets of the Dracadia Guild. Devryce and Lilia's story is one of obsession, biological mysteries, and a sickness that runs deeper than the bone.

On this remote island, the lines between professor and student, cure and poison, and love and madness are irrevocably blurred. Some truths are meant to stay buried in the fungal soil of Nocticadia.
