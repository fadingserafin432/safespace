Description

Fairydale by Veronica Lancet is a dark, atmospheric romance set in a secluded town filled with secrets. When the heroine arrives in Fairydale, she finds herself drawn into a web of mystery tied to her past and to a brooding, powerful man who seems connected to the town’s shadowed history.

As hidden truths surface, the story explores fate, obsession, trauma, and a love that blurs the line between salvation and destruction. Enter the haunting world of Fairydale, where ancient folklore breathes life into the shadows of a secluded village.
