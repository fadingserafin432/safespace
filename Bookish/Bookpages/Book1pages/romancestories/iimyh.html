The Story

If It Makes You Happy by Julie Olivia is a contemporary romance novel about healing, friendship, and finding love again in unexpected places...

Set in the cozy autumn of 1997, it explores themes like grief, personal growth, and choosing happiness over expectations. Readers love this slow-burn friends-to-lovers tale for its heart and small-town warmth.
